## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary**: Mosque Administrators (DKM) and Amil Zakat. Their goal is efficient management of mosque finances, ZIS (Zakat, Infaq, Shadaqah), inventory, and community events.
- **Secondary**: Jamaah (Mosque Community). They use the platform to check prayer times, upcoming events, and fundraising progress through the Public Portal.

### Brand Personality
- **Modern**: Clean layouts, high-quality typography, and smooth interactions (Framer Motion).
- **Spiritual**: Respectful, calm, and grounded. Uses cultural cues like "Ahlan wa Sahlan" and traditional color accents (Emerald/Amber).
- **Professional**: Trustworthy and data-driven. Executive dashboards with financial insights to ensure transparency and accountability.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: A unique blend of **Neo-Brutalist** (hard shadows, bold borders, high radius `rounded-[2.5rem]`) and **Editorial Elegance** (Playfair Display headers, Stone-based warm neutrals).
- **References**: Inspired by the clean, authoritative, and organized feel of [muhammadiyah.or.id](https://muhammadiyah.or.id/).
- **Theme**: Prioritizes a **Warm Stone Light Theme** (`--background: 40 20% 98%`) to evoke a sense of calm, natural warmth, and accessibility.
- **Anti-References**: Avoids "AI Slop" (generic cyan-on-dark, purple-to-blue gradients, or overused Inter-only SaaS layouts).

### Design Principles
1. **Calm Clarity**: Use the Warm Stone palette to reduce cognitive load and create a welcoming environment for both admins and jamaah.
2. **Bold Hierarchy**: Use the `font-display` (Playfair Display) for large, italic, black-weight headers to create a strong, memorable editorial identity.
3. **Illustrative Solidity**: Employ the Neo-Brutalist card style (`.illustrative-card`) with hard shadows and thick borders to communicate durability and institutional strength.
4. **Proactive Insight**: The interface should not just display data but provide actionable "Executive Insights" (e.g., alerts for idle cash or financial deficits).
5. **Cultural Resonance**: Incorporate appropriate spiritual terminology and aesthetic choices that align with the mosque context without sacrificing modern SaaS usability.
